Vada

Description

Vada is a savory fried snack from South India, made from ground urad dal and seasoned with spices. It is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, typically served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • Urad dal
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Curry leaves
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Soak Dal: Soak 1 cup of urad dal in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind: Drain the dal and grind it to a smooth, thick batter, adding as little water as possible.
  3. Add Spices: Mix chopped green chilies, grated ginger, curry leaves, black pepper, and salt into the batter.
  4. Shape Vadas: Wet your hands, take a small portion of the batter, and shape it into a round patty with a hole in the center.
  5. Fry: Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the vadas until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Serve: Serve the vadas hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
